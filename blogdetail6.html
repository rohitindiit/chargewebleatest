Top 10 Reasons Why EV Owners Should Have the ChargEasy App

19 Dec 2023

Introduction

Electric vehicles (EVs) are becoming increasingly popular, and for good reason. They're better for the environment, cheaper to operate than gas-powered cars, and offer a number of other benefits. However, one of the biggest challenges for EV owners is finding and using charging stations. That's where ChargEasy comes in. ChargEasy is the EV charging app that makes it easy to find and use charging stations near you.

Here are the top 10 reasons why EV owners should have the ChargEasy app:

1. Find charging stations easily

ChargEasy makes it easy to find charging stations near you. You can search by location, availability, and price. You can also filter your search results by charging speed, connector type, and other criteria.

2. Get real-time information on charging station status

ChargEasy gives you real-time information on the status of charging stations. This includes information on whether the station is available, how many stalls are open, and the current charging speed. This information can help you to plan your charging stops more effectively.

3. Pay for charging sessions directly from the app

With ChargEasy, you can pay for charging sessions directly from the app, using your smartphone. This eliminates the need to carry cash or credit cards.

4. Set charging schedules

ChargEasy allows you to set charging schedules to take advantage of lower electricity rates. This can save you money on your electricity bills.

5. Receive notifications when your EV is fully charged

ChargEasy can send you notifications when your EV is fully charged. This helps you to avoid overcharging your batteries.

6. Earn rewards for charging your EV

ChargEasy offers a rewards program that allows you to earn points for charging your EV. These points can be redeemed for discounts on charging fees, gift cards, and other rewards.

7. Support the transition to a more sustainable future

By using the ChargEasy app, you are supporting the transition to a more sustainable future. EVs are better for the environment than gasoline-powered cars, and the ChargEasy app makes it easier to own and use an EV.

8. Save time and money

ChargEasy can save you time and money by helping you to find charging stations quickly and easily. You can also use ChargEasy to take advantage of lower electricity rates and earn rewards for charging your EV.

9. Reduce range anxiety

Range anxiety is a common concern among EV owners, but the ChargEasy app can help to reduce this anxiety by showing you the nearest charging stations and their availability.

10. Be part of a community

The ChargEasy app connects EV owners with each other and with the broader EV community. This can be a great way to learn more about EVs, share tips and advice, and connect with other EV owners who share your passion for sustainability.

Conclusion

The ChargEasy app is a must-have for EV owners. It's the most convenient and affordable way to find and use charging stations near you. Download the ChargEasy app today and start enjoying the benefits of EV ownership!

-Kelly A Norton
